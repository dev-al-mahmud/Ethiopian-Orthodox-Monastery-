Our Sacred Purpose

Our Sacred Mission

Rooted in the ancient traditions of the Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church, our monastery serves as a beacon of faith, scholarship, and compassion.

Faith

Upholding the ancient traditions of the Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church.

Prayer

Community

Through the Centuries

A Legacy of Faith

6th Century

The Sacred Origins

Founded by devout fathers who sought the solitude of the Ethiopian highlands, our monastery began as a humble sanctuary of prayer. Through divine inspiration and unwavering faith, a community of monks established a sacred space that would endure for centuries.

12th Century

Spiritual Legacy

The fathers and mothers who kept the faith alive through periods of both prosperity and trial. Their devotion carved not only stone but the very soul of our tradition, creating a spiritual legacy that guides us to this day.

Present Day

Cultural Preservation

Protecting ancient manuscripts and sacred art, our monastery continues its mission of preservation. We safeguard thousands of Geez manuscripts, illuminated icons, and liturgical objects — living testimonies of our unbroken heritage.
